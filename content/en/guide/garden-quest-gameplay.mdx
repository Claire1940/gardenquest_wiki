<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Core Farming Loop and Crop Mechanics">

The foundation of garden quest gameplay lies in its agricultural engine. Players begin with a small plot of land, basic seeds, and standard watering tools. The progression relies on a simple four-step cycle: soil preparation, seed planting, hydration management, and harvesting. Maximizing your silver-per-minute ratio requires a clear understanding of crop growth times and market values.

Turnips serve as the primary starter crop. Early footage confirms that turnip seeds are cheap to purchase and quick to mature, making them ideal for rapid reinvestment. As players level up their farming proficiency, they unlock higher-tier seeds that require longer growth periods but yield significantly higher market returns.

<Callout type="tip" title="Hydration Management">
Do not let your crop plots dry out. Under-watered crops experience severe growth-rate penalties, which delays your harvest cycles and lowers overall profit efficiency.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Companion Pets and Fishing Systems">

Pets in Garden Quest are more than simple cosmetic followers; they are active gameplay multipliers that directly impact your farm's efficiency. Players can equip up to three pets simultaneously. These companions provide passive buffs such as increased crop yields, faster movement speeds, or higher chances for crops to undergo rare mutations.

The Mythic Unicorn stands out as an elite companion. According to preview data, the Unicorn doubles the probability of plants and fruits mutating into their highly valuable Rainbow variants. Pairing the Unicorn with other support pets, such as the Deer or the Robin, creates a powerful synergy that maximizes the value of every single harvest.

<Callout type="info" title="Mutation Mechanics">
Rainbow crops sell for a massive premium at the market. Utilizing mutation-boosting pets is the most reliable method for generating massive end-game wealth.
</Callout>

For players looking to break up the monotony of farming, the fishing system offers a highly rewarding side activity. Located near the coastal docks and inland ponds, fishing allows players to catch unique aquatic species that can be sold for silver or donated to collection logs.
