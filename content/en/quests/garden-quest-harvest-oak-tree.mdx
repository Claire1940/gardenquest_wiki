<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step: How to Harvest Oak Trees Efficiently">

Chopping hardwood requires more than just standing next to a trunk and holding down the action button. To get the most out of every tree, you should follow a structured approach that conserves your character's energy and maximizes wood recovery.

By organizing your woodcutting sessions, you can clear entire groves quickly and establish a highly profitable harvesting routine.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip the Correct Axe">
    Open your inventory and equip your highest-tier axe. Ensure your character is not holding seeds or fishing rods, as this will prevent the woodcutting action from triggering.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate a Mature Oak Tree">
    Travel to the Whispering Woods or Forest Outskirts. Look for trees with dark brown bark and broad green canopies. Avoid small saplings, as they do not yield oak logs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Position and Strike">
    Stand directly adjacent to the oak trunk. Click or tap the tree to initiate the swing. Watch the swing meter and time your clicks to hit the green sweet spot for bonus damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect Logs and Saplings">
    Once the oak tree falls, collect the dropped Oak Logs, Oak Saplings, and occasional acorns. Saplings can be replanted on your personal plot for a sustainable wood source.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The swing meter mechanic is particularly important. Hitting the sweet spot consistently can reduce the total number of swings required to fell a tree by up to forty percent. This mechanic rewards active gameplay and prevents woodcutting from becoming a purely passive, repetitive chore.

<Callout type="warning" title="Energy Management">
Chopping oak trees consumes stamina. If your energy bar reaches zero, your swing speed drops by 50%. Keep food items on hand to quickly replenish your stamina.
</Callout>

</Section>
