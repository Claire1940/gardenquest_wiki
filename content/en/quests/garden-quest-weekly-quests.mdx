<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Quest Optimization Route">

To complete your weekly rotation without spending hours grinding, follow this optimized route. This sequence minimizes travel time between your personal garden plot, the central town, and the fishing ponds.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check the Quest Board Immediately Post-Reset">
    As soon as the weekly reset occurs, open your quest log to review the requirements. Group similar tasks together (e.g., if you have both a fishing quest and a water-crop harvesting quest, plan to do them in the same run).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prepare Your Agricultural Plots">
    Clear your active soil and plant the specific seeds required for the weekly board. If the quest demands slow-growing, high-value crops, plant them first so they can mature in the background while you complete active tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Travel to the Fishing and Gathering Zones">
    While your crops are growing, head to the local water sources. Use this time to complete any fishing objectives. This ensures you are constantly progressing rather than standing idle waiting for plants to grow.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Visit the Gear Shop and NPC Hubs">
    Once your bags are full of fish and harvested crops, head to the central town. Turn in completed objectives to the respective NPCs, buy any required gear upgrades, and purchase replacement seeds for your next planting cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>
