To help you hit the ground running on launch day, we have mapped out the optimal starter quest route. Following this sequence ensures you do not waste early-game currency on inefficient upgrades.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accept the Starter Quest">
    Spawn into the main village and speak to the Head Gardener NPC. They will hand you your first packet of turnip seeds and a basic watering can.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prepare and Plant Your Plot">
    Head over to your designated garden plot. Interact with the dirt tiles to plant your turnip seeds. Turnips are the fastest-growing starter crop, making them perfect for rapid quest completion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Water and Nurture">
    Equip your watering can and water each planted tile. Keep an eye on the soil hydration levels; dry crops will stop growing, delaying your quest rewards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Harvest and Deliver">
    Once the turnips reach full maturity, harvest them and return to the Head Gardener. Delivering these crops completes your first major quest, rewarding you with coins and unlocking the Gear Shop.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Turnip Farming Meta">
