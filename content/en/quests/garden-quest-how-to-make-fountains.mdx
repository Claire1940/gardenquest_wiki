<Section icon={Target} title="Garden Quest How to Make Fountains: Step-by-Step Setup">

Building a custom water feature or placing a pre-made asset requires a clear understanding of the game's placement grid and structural mechanics. Follow this step-by-step walkthrough to master the process of garden quest how to make fountains on your customized plot.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Decoration Menu">
    Enter your player plot and open the main building interface. Navigate to the housing and decor tab to view your stored blueprints, purchased fountains, and structural building blocks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear a Flat Foundation Area">
    Fountains require a stable, unobstructed grid space to place correctly. Clear out any wild rocks, weeds, or stray crop plots. Ensure the selected area is completely flat before initiating placement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Assemble the Outer Basin (DIY Method)">
    If you are building a custom fountain, select modular stone walls from your inventory. Arrange them in a symmetrical circular or square pattern to form the main water containment basin.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Install the Water Spout Emitter">
    Place a functional water spout or emitter block directly in the center of your constructed basin. This item generates the dynamic flowing water animation essential for a realistic fountain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Apply Lighting and Decorative Accents">
    Surround the fountain base with decorative lights, flower beds, or seating objects. Adding neon or warm lights inside the water basin creates a stunning visual effect during night cycles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Grid Alignment Notice">
Ensure your grid-snapping tool is enabled in the settings menu. Placing modular fountain blocks without grid-snapping can result in asymmetrical layouts and water emitter misalignment, which disrupts the flow animation.
</Callout>

Custom DIY builds offer unparalleled creative freedom. By combining different stone types and water spouts, you can design multi-tiered structures that rival the default shop blueprints. Use the table below to plan your modular material purchases.
