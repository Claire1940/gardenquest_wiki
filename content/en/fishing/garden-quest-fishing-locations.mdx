<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Fishing & Use Bait">

The fishing loop is a standalone system designed to complement your overall garden progression. Unlike crop farming, which requires waiting for seeds to mature, fishing offers an active gameplay alternative that provides immediate currency and upgrade materials. Follow these steps to unlock the system and start catching fish on launch day.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Visit the Gear Shop">
    Locate the Gear Shop NPC near the central plaza to browse the available utility tools and starter rods.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Purchase the Basic Rod">
    Spend your starting copper coins to purchase the Basic Fishing Rod and a small batch of Earthworms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip Your Gear">
    Open your player inventory, equip the rod to your active tool slot, and select your desired bait.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Cast and Reel">
    Approach any valid water source, click to cast, and wait for the bobber to submerge before reeling.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Using the correct bait is critical for targeting high-value species. While fishing without bait is possible, it significantly reduces your bite rate and limits your catches to common tier items. Refer to the table below to plan your bait purchases at the Gear Shop.

| Bait Type | Cost (Coins) | Attraction Bonus | Mutation Rate |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Earthworm | 5 | +10% Bite Rate | 0% |
| Glowing Grub | 15 | +25% Bite Rate | +5% Rainbow |
| Golden Beetle | 50 | +50% Bite Rate | +15% Rainbow |

<Callout type="tip" title="Bait Conservation">
Do not waste high-tier baits like Golden Beetles in the Starter Pond. Save them for the Hidden Lake where high-value species reside to maximize your return on investment.
</Callout>

</Section>
