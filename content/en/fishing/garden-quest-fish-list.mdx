<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Fishing Rods, Bait, and Gear Shop Upgrades">

To catch the most elusive creatures on the **garden quest fish list**, you cannot rely on basic equipment. The local Gear Shop is your primary destination for upgrading your fishing utility. Upgrading your rod increases your casting distance, speeds up the reeling minigame, and unlocks the ability to reel in heavier, higher-rarity fish.

| Rod Tier | Shop Cost | Key Benefit | Recommended Level |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| Splintered Rod | Free (Starter Quest) | Basic casting, Common fish only | Level 1 |
| Bamboo Rod | 500 Coins | +15% Cast Distance, Faster bite time | Level 5 |
| Fiberglass Rod | 2500 Coins | Unlocks Rare fish, wider reel sweet-spot | Level 12 |
| Carbon Fiber Rod | 12000 Coins | Unlocks Epic fish, +30% Cast Distance | Level 20 |
| Golden Rod | 50000 Coins | Unlocks Legendary/Mythic fish, Max stats | Level 30 |

Bait is another crucial component of the fishing loop. While you can fish without bait, using specialized lures will target specific species and filter out low-value catches.

| Bait Type | Source | Target Tier | Special Effect |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| Breadcrumbs | Crafting / Shop | Common | Reduces bite wait time |
| Earthworms | Tilling Soil | Uncommon | Boosts Uncommon catch rate |
| Shiny Spoon | Gear Shop | Rare | Attracts predatory fish |
| Glow-Worm | Cave Harvesting | Epic | Required for deep-water species |
| Rainbow Bait | Mythic Pet Perk | Legendary+ | Doubles chance of mutation |

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Fishing Guide for Beginners">

If you want to maximize your efficiency the moment the servers go live, follow this optimized day-one fishing route.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Your Starter Rod">
    Complete the first three introductory farming quests on your plot. Once you harvest your first turnip crop, speak to the Elder NPC near the town square to receive your free Splintered Rod.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Starter Pond">
    Walk directly south of your main farm plot to find the starter pond. This area is safe and does not require any level gates or bridge repairs to access.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip Bait and Cast">
    Open your inventory, equip the Splintered Rod, and select any basic bait you gathered while clearing weeds from your plot. Click or tap on the water to cast your line.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Master the Reel Minigame">
    When the bobber sinks, tap immediately to hook the fish. Keep the tension bar in the green sweet-spot by tapping rhythmically until the progress bar fills up.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Sell and Reinvest">
    Take your catches to the local fish merchant next to the Gear Shop. Sell your common catches to fund your first major tool upgrades.
  </Step>
</Steps>
