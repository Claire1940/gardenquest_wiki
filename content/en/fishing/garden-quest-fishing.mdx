<Section icon={Target} title="How to Fish: Step-by-Step Angling Guide">

Mastering the mechanics of the water requires patience and precision. The activity is structured around an interactive mini-game that rewards timing and gear preparation. By understanding the loop before launch day, you can avoid wasting valuable early-game currency on inefficient attempts.

Follow these standard operational steps to ensure a successful catch on every cast:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain a Starter Rod">
    Visit the local merchant to purchase your first basic fishing rod. This entry-level tool allows you to access basic water bodies but has limited reach and durability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Appropriate Bait">
    Select your bait from your inventory menu. Different bait types attract specific classes of aquatic life, directly influencing your target pool.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate an Active Fishing Node">
    Search the shores for visible ripples or splashing water. Casting near active nodes significantly reduces the time it takes for a bite to occur.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Cast">
    Hold down the action button to charge your casting meter. Release it when the gauge reaches the optimal zone to land your hook directly in the target area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Complete the Reeling Mini-Game">
    Once a bite is registered, keep the tension bar within the designated green safety zone by tapping or holding the reel button until the catch is secured.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Patience Pays Off">
Rushing the reel mechanism or ignoring the tension bar will cause the line to snap, resulting in the loss of both your bait and the potential catch. Always prioritize steady rhythm over rapid tapping.
</Callout>

Each successful capture rewards you with fish that can either be sold directly at the market or saved for specific quests. As you progress, the complexity of the reeling mini-game increases, demanding better equipment to handle more aggressive aquatic species.
